Tasked with storing the transformed datasets into a database. The database system of choice was postgres SQL. 

REASONS FOR CHOOSING POSTGRES SQL

ease of use
ability to easily import our CSV files into tables
Very straightforward
Intuitive

It offers amazing support for our growing data. We found ourselves on this path of trying to create multiple efficient tables with
